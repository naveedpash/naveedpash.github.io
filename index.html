Bariatric Surgeries

There are several types of bariatric surgeries with the most common being sleeve gastrectomy and one-anastomosis gastric bypass. Which surgery you will get depends on various factors that you will decide in guidance with your surgeon and nutritionist. Click here to learn more...

Nutrition for Bariatric Patients

Nutrition is an essential component for obesity patients. Nutritional strategies can be used as a tool for weight loss without surgery. Nutrition becomes especially important after bariatric surgery to maintain weight loss as well as to avoid critical deficiencies of minerals and vitamins.
